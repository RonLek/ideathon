ABOUT HACKTHON 2k21

A great idea requires a healthy environment to be turned into a reality. E-Cell at IIIT Pune comes forth with its flagship event - HACKATHON - a design sprint-like event in which computer programmers or anyone involved in software development collaborate and work on problems that itch their brains. We are glad to offer you an opportunity to connect with like-minded people and combine your enthusiasm levels to come up with authentic software solutions.
